Announcing Our Modern Laboratory & Superior Patient Care Facilities!

Laminar Operation Theatre

A Laminar Flow Operation Theatre is a highly specialized surgical room designed to maintain a sterile environment. It uses advanced air filtration systems to create a continuous flow of clean, particle-free air over the surgical site. This helps to minimize the risk of infection and ensures optimal conditions for delicate surgical procedures.

DR X-Ray

DR X-ray is a revolutionary imaging technique that significantly enhances diagnostic accuracy. By providing high-resolution images, faster acquisition times, and reduced radiation exposure, DR X-ray empowers healthcare professionals to make informed decisions and deliver optimal patient care.

C-Arm

A C-Arm is a type of X-ray machine that is used to obtain real-time images during surgical procedures. It consists of an X-ray source and a detector mounted on a C-shaped arm that can be rotated around the patient. This allows for dynamic imaging, enabling surgeons to visualize the surgical site from multiple angles. C-Arms are commonly used in orthopedic, vascular, and interventional radiology procedures.

Ultra-Sound Scan

Ultrasound scans are a safe and painless imaging technique that uses sound waves to create images of your internal organs. No radiation is involved, making it safe for people of all ages, including pregnant women. The procedure is quick and often requires no special preparation. Your healthcare provider will explain the specific details of your scan and any necessary instructions.

Laproscopy

Laparoscopic surgery is a minimally invasive surgical technique that involves making small incisions to perform surgical procedures. This method often leads to faster recovery times, less pain, and smaller scars compared to traditional open surgery.

ETO sterilisation unit

we prioritize patient safety and infection control with our state-of-the-art ETO Sterilization Unit. This advanced facility ensures the safe and effective sterilization of medical instruments, surgical tools, and heat-sensitive equipment using Ethylene Oxide (ETO) gas. Our sterilization process eliminates bacteria, viruses, and fungi while maintaining the integrity of delicate materials. With strict safety protocols and compliance with industry standards, we provide a reliable and efficient sterilization solution to support
high-quality patient care.

ICU with ventilator facility

Our hospital's Intensive Care Unit (ICU) is equipped with advanced ventilator support to provide critical care for patients with severe respiratory conditions and life-threatening illnesses. Our highly trained medical team ensures round-the-clock monitoring, personalized treatment, and emergency response. With state-of-the-art technology and a compassionate approach, we are committed to delivering the highest standard of care to stabilize and support patients in critical conditions.

Centralised oxygen facility

Our hospital is equipped with a state-of-the-art centralized oxygen supply system, ensuring a constant and reliable flow of medical-grade oxygen to all critical care units, operation theaters, and patient wards. This system enhances patient safety, eliminates the need for individual oxygen cylinders, and provides uninterrupted oxygen support for those in need. With advanced monitoring and backup mechanisms, we guarantee efficient and safe oxygen delivery for superior patient care.

keyhole surgery facility

we offer advanced keyhole surgery (minimally invasive surgery) for faster recovery and minimal scarring. Using state-of-the-art laparoscopic techniques, our expert surgeons perform procedures with small incisions, reducing pain, hospital stays, and complications. Whether for gallbladder removal, hernia repair, or other specialized treatments, our keyhole surgery ensures precision, safety, and quicker return to daily life.

Biphasic defibrillator

A biphasic defibrillator is an advanced medical device used to deliver controlled electric shocks to restore normal heart rhythm in patients experiencing cardiac arrest or life-threatening arrhythmias. Unlike traditional monophasic defibrillators, biphasic technology delivers energy in two phases, making it more effective at lower energy levels, reducing damage to the heart. This ensures higher success rates in resuscitation while minimizing complications. Our hospital is equipped with state-of-the-art biphasic defibrillators, ensuring prompt and efficient emergency cardiac care for our patients.

Daycare surgery

our Daycare Surgery Unit provides safe and efficient same-day surgical procedures, allowing patients to return home within hours of their operation. Our expert medical team utilizes advanced techniques to ensure minimal discomfort, faster recovery, and reduced hospital stays. We specialize in a wide range of daycare procedures, including general, orthopedic, gynecological, and ENT surgeries. With state-of-the-art facilities and personalized care, we prioritize your comfort and well-being. Experience hassle-free, high-quality surgical care with us.
